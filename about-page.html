About Streemly

Streemly is a company working to improve the decision and approval processes in the workforce. They are building a chrome extension to facilitate this. The final complete product will streamline approval processes saving comapnies time and money. This rule engine is one piece of the extension that will allow approval requests to be routed to the correct people in the organization automatically, vased on values in the form.
