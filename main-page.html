Introducing the Rule Engine

Rule engine is a special rule that deals with complex decisions and problems that no company would waste their time and energy. It helps route approval requests to the correct recipients in an organize logical algorithmic procedure. An app that makes difficult decisions simple and effective.


We're collaborating with Streemly as a chrome extension to it's lightning speed process.


A form of perpetual motion machine. It's like reinventing the wheel in a new conceptual way...and it's
all on your app.



Our company collaborator

Streemly

Streemly is a company working to improve the decision and approval processes in the workforce.
They are building a chrome extension to facilitate this. The final complete product will streamline
approval processes saving comapnies time and money. This rule engine is one piece of the extension
that will allow approval requests to be routed to the correct people in the organization automatically,
based on values in the form.
